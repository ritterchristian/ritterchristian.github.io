About Me

Hey there! Thanks for stopping on by! My name's Christian Ritter. This is me standing on Cadillac Mountain at Mount Desert Island, Bar Harbor, Maine (ignore the sweat stains, sorry).

I love exploring...

...especially with friends,...

...photography...

...Virginia Tech...

...and especially my dogs.

After spending my last three and a half years in Blacksburg, I've come to truly enjoy the beautiful scenery of Southwest Virginia and the abundant things to do around here. I've put together this website to shed a little light into the things that are available for students and residents to do around here. There are pictures and videos of hikes and parks in Blacksburg and surrounding towns, all in hopes to get people outside! I hope you enjoy!
