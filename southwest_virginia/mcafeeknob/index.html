McAfee Knob

McAfee Knob is an 8.3 mile hike in Catawba, Virginia, about 45 minutes out from Blacksburg and Virginia Tech. It's one of the most popular and photographed hikes not only in Southwest Virginia but also on the whole Appalachian Trail. So, don't be surprised to see a lot of people when you're hiking, especially if the weather's nice!

Parking: If you're on VA-311 coming from VA-785 N, the parking lot will be about 1.5 up the road on the right.

From the parking lot, the trail to start the hike will be across the street. *Watch out while you're crossing the street... Cars can be going very quickly around the bends.*
